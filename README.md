## How to include the badge in your project 

Simply copy and paste the following code into your README, and replace `YOUR_LINK_HERE` with your PayPal link! 😌

```html
<a href="YOUR_LINK_HERE">
  <picture>
    <source media="(prefers-color-scheme: dark)" srcset="[https://github.com/R0mb0/Support_the_dev_badge/blob/main/Badge/SVG/Support_the_dev_badge_Dark.svg](https://github.com/R0mb0/Support_the_dev_badge/blob/main/Badge/SVG/Support_the_dev_badge_Dark.svg)">
```

## Story of this repository

At the time of creating this repository, I am a working university student who does his best to release as many tools open-source as possible. Unfortunately, balancing work and studies means my financial situation is quite precarious. 

Looking at my GitHub statistics, I noticed that my tools are actually being used by many people. However, I also realized that no one had ever made a donation, even just as a small token of appreciation for the open-source work I do. So, I decided to design this badge to gently highlight the possibility of supporting my work! ʕっ•ᴥ•ʔっ
